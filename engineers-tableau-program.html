Tableau Engineers Program (TEP)

While the universities feel that they have trained their students for their future workplaces in various domains, many a times it has been observed by experts that the industry demands are not met by the university trainings. In fact, there exists a gap where the level of employability skills of graduates and entry level work requirements do not meet. Besides this, on an average, only 10% to 20% of freshers actually are skilled to apply their coding/programming knowledge of what they have learnt at the time of Engineering.

At Dataastic, the goal is to teach the student how a theoretical tableau feature/concept is applied in real life or Industry scenarios. Once you undergo the TEP at Dataastic, it’s not only a reporting tool you will master but, the holistic concepts of Business Intelligence, Data warehousing will be crystal clear to you to crack any interview in Data Engineering domain. The syllabus is aligned to Industry standards.

This course is live, instructor-led & helps you master concepts like Blending, Dimensions/Measures to name a few, with industry use cases. Completion of TEP successfully at Dataastic would be a visa for you to enter into Data Science world.

The value addition this program would bring in is assistance with the Tableau certification. We also focus on other aspects like interview readiness, problem solving skills, certification and many more, thus ensuring a holistic development for the candidate which furthers the prospect of job placements.
